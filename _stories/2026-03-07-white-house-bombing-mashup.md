Hours after the New York Times reported that US strikes had killed children in an Iranian elementary school, the White House's official social-media account posted a montage video mixing airstrike footage with clips from Top Gun, Iron Man, Ghostbusters, and other action films. The juxtaposition was not anonymous and not satirical: it appeared on the institutional feed.

Whether intended as celebration, deflection, or test of audience reaction, the choice rendered the strike as cinema. The dead children were the absent center of the joke.
